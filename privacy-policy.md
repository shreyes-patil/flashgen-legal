FlashGen Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 28, 2024

FlashGen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address for authentication purposes.

Flashcard Data: We store the flashcard sets you create, including topics, questions, answers, difficulty levels, and practice history.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, such as when you last reviewed a flashcard set.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the FlashGen service
- Authenticate your account and keep it secure
- Generate flashcard content using AI (OpenAI)
- Save and sync your flashcard sets across sessions
- Improve our app and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure.
All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Sign-In: For authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
OpenAI API: To generate flashcard content from your input topics (subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy)
Supabase: For secure cloud data storage (subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy)

When you use FlashGen, your topic inputs are sent to OpenAI's API to generate flashcard content. Please review OpenAI's privacy policy to understand how they handle this data.

We retain your account information and flashcard data for as long as your account is active.
